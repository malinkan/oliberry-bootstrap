About

Welcome! My name is Tasha. I developed a love for all things creative as far back as I can remember. My grandparents had a beautiful cottage by the forest in Ural Mountains. The happiest chilhood memoris I have is about walking the fields, playing with flowers in the garden, drawing people and nature.

My career path took me in a completely different direction, when I discovered my talent for math and going to a technical school seemed more reasonable. And even though it was a fun time working in a software engineering field, full of incredible experiences and learnings, it always felt that there have been something always missing and I am not doing what I felt is right for me. After taking some time of work and doing some much needed soulsearching, I have decided to trust my inner calling and reconnect with a creative part of self.

I have created this website with a sole purpose to match myself with a Floristic Studio, where my skils, creativity and personality could be of service and also learn and explore further my floristic talent.

Art

Over the last 2 years I have been reconnecting with my passion for drawing and found a greate pleasure in expressing myself through paintings. I have been lucky to live and travel through beautiful places in Europe and North America. The inspiration is everywhere - walks in the Red woods of California, hiking in Rocky and Karpatian Mountains, gazing the sunsets in endless fields of central Ukraine, my home country, biking through ceneries of Itally and Spain, contemplating ever-changing state of Atlantic ocean. But most of all my inspiration is coming from living life, having hear-to-heart conversations with my friends, exploring the ways of overcoming the everyday chalenges on our jorner to wholness.
